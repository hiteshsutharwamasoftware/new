## Description

Homepage visitors (both new and returning users) need a textbox on the homepage to input information or interact with the application directly from the landing page. This feature streamlines user engagement by allowing users to immediately enter data or search, improving usability and reducing friction. The textbox should be prominently placed and accessible on all devices, ensuring a seamless experience.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Given I am on the homepage, When the page loads, Then I should see a textbox displayed prominently.
- [ ] Given I am viewing the homepage on a mobile device, When the page loads, Then the textbox should be visible and usable without horizontal scrolling.
- [ ] Given I am on the homepage, When I click into the textbox, Then I should be able to enter text without errors.
